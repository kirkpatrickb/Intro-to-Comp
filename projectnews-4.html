WHAT’S NEW IN VALORANT EPISODE 5 ACT II

Episode 5 Act II is where we crown the fearless competitor and reach new heights together. Clutch your share of the throne and wear it on your sleeve with the latest Battlepass, limited edition Champions skinline, and new Event Pass.

Watch Champs, play VALORANT, take a piece of the glory with you. Play and earn XP to add free exclusive items like a Player Card, Spray, Gun Buddy, and more to your collection:

NEW SKINLINE: CHAMPIONS 2022 COLLECTION (AUG. 23)

Play like a champ with a loadout that evolves the more you add to your body count. Come up short, and you’ll lose that championship aura. The Champions-themed Phantom changes every five kills (max 25), and the butterfly knife has its own tricks too—if you’re good enough to secure the frag.

Also from Aug. 23 to Sept. 21, 50% of net proceeds from the Champions 2022 Collection will go to participating teams. This bundle (which also includes a Player Card, Gun Buddy, and Spray) will be deadstock in no time, and it won’t return to the rotating Store or Night.Market.

Can you tell we’re excited for Champions? To keep the theme going the Act II Battlepass centers around myths and legends. You know, the kind you’ll tell your grandkid when they're playing on Patch 55.02.

Purchase the Act II Premium Battlepass to earn some extra good stuff, like the Obsidian and Immortalized Vandal, Hehehehehe Spray, or the Kitsune Mask Gun Buddy.

Don’t go acting like you’ve mastered Pearl yet or earned the Ascendent ranks. Get out there and reach new heights.
